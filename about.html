What We Do

Third Earth Surfboards handles everything from everyday dings to more serious board damage, restorations, and custom board builds.

Whether your board needs a quick repair, a cleaner finish, or a complete custom build, the goal is simple: solid work, honest pricing, and boards that get back in the water.

Repair Process

1. Send PhotosText or email pictures of the damage, board type, and any details you know.
2. Get A QuotePricing depends on board material, damage size, location, and finish work needed.
3. Drop Off BoardSchedule a time, drop it off, and the repair work begins after inspection.

Custom Boards & Restorations

Third Earth Surfboards also offers custom surfboard builds from raw foam blanks, custom epoxy construction, restorations, and vintage board work.

For custom work, send photos, board dimensions, riding style, and what you want the finished board to feel like.
